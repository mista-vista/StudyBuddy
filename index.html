How It Works

Up and running in minutes

From download to your first study session — the whole process is designed to be fast and friction-free.

1

Download & Sign In

Install the APK and sign in with your Google account. No passwords to remember.

2

Verify Your ID

Scan your QCU Student ID with the camera. The app reads your name and student number automatically.

3

Set Your Subjects

Choose subjects you can teach and subjects you need help with. Rate your confidence per subject.

4

Get Matched & Connect

The algorithm finds mutual matches. Send a wave, start chatting, and start learning together.

The Problem

Many students struggle with self-paced learning and feel disconnected when studying in isolation — even with AI tools, there's a lack of real human-to-human engagement for complex concepts.

The Solution

StudyBuddy uses a competency-based matching algorithm to instantly connect students with peers who excel in the areas they struggle with — and vice versa, creating genuinely mutual partnerships.

Tech Stack

Built with React Native (Expo), Firebase Firestore, Firebase Auth, and real-time presence tracking. OCR-powered ID verification via camera. Deployed as a native Android APK.
